Thanks for your interest in my project Remember It Wholesale.

The purpose of this app is to turn your life into usable data, in any arbitrary form you want. You can store Things of milestones, reminders, goals, events, memories, and more.

Examples
Personally I'm terrible at remembering birthdays, or what boardgame I last played with a group of friends (or even the food I served them). I'm also interested in personal trends like how many times I drove to work compared to walking in a year. Or maybe I need help choosing a movie to watch or a pub to go to. Even boring life stuff like when I bought my fridge to check when the warranty runs out.
These are some examples of the limitless types of data that can be organized and tracked with custom Templates in the app.

How the Demo Works
Feel free to check out the app for the entire day, and start a new demo later if needed.
The demo login information will be stored in your browser, so you can leave your session and come back later.

Note that once you logout (or the demo ends) all your data and things will be cleaned up.
